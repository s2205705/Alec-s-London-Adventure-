Alec's London Story

Alec arrived in London as a university student, wide-eyed and ready to explore every corner of this historic city. His adventure began at the university campus, where he met friends from around the world who would join him on his explorations.

From studying at the library to late-night walks across London Bridge, Alec discovered that the city had layers upon layers of history and culture. He'd spend weekends getting lost in Central London, discovering hidden gems between the major landmarks.

The Natural History Museum became his favorite study spot, where he'd write essays surrounded by dinosaurs. Liverpool Street station marked the beginning of countless adventures, while Bermondsey Street showed him London's creative side with its independent shops and cafes.

His most breathtaking discovery was Horizon 22, where he saw London from heights he'd never imagined. Each location in this gallery represents a chapter in Alec's ongoing London adventure.
